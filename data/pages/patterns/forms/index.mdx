## Overview

Form patterns provide standardized approaches to common form design challenges, ensuring consistency and usability across INFORM applications.

### Key Principles

- **Progressive disclosure**: Break complex forms into manageable steps
- **Clear validation**: Provide immediate, helpful feedback
- **Accessibility first**: Ensure forms work for all users
- **Mobile responsive**: Optimize for all screen sizes
