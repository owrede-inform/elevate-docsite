## Overview

Data display patterns help users quickly understand and act on information presented in your application.

### Key Principles

- **Scannable layout**: Make information easy to scan and digest
- **Progressive disclosure**: Show essential data first, details on demand
- **Consistent formatting**: Use standard formats for dates, numbers, and text
- **Actionable insights**: Make it easy to act on displayed data
